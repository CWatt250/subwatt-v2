How to use
1
Pick a destination
Click any county on the map, or type a city in the search bar. SubWatt picks the local that covers it and auto-selects the nearest dispatch point. The big $X/day at the top of the result card is your rate.
2
Browse locals in the sidebar
Every HFIAW local is listed on the left. Click a card to expand it and reveal its dispatch buttons. Only one card is open at a time. The sidebar scrolls if you have more locals than fit.
3
Compare dispatch points
With a destination already selected, click any other dispatch button — even from a different local — to reroute and recompute the rate. Only the active dispatch's zone rings show on the map; old rings clear automatically. The map view stays put so you don't lose your job site.
4
Irex Argus branches
Hit the Irex Branches button in the top bar to show the 9 Argus locations as blue square markers. Click any marker, then USE AS DISPATCH in the popup, to route from that branch instead — SubWatt still applies the local rate that covers your destination county.
5
Hanford Site
Colored polygons inside Benton County (100/200/300/400 areas) use the flat HSSA rate instead of distance zones.
6
Edit miles
The miles field in the Daily Cost Calculator auto-fills from the road route — type a different number to override it.
7
Export PDF
Use the Export PDF button in the top bar to save the current view as a PDF.
Rates are estimates — always verify with your CBA
